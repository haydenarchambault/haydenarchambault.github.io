1908 Race Riot AR

1908 Race Riot AR was created by STUDIO Amakawa. The application is based at the Springfield 1908 Race Riot National Historic Monument to be used alongside the walking tour located in Springfield, Illinois. My roles included programming and design using Unity.

Download the app on Google Play or the App Store for more information.

Heart Mountain AR

Heart Mountain AR was created by STUDIO Amakawa. The application was selected as winner of the 2022 Wyoming State Historical Society's first place award for activities. It is based in Cody, Wyoming at the site of the Heart Mountain Relocation Center National Historic Landmark. My roles included programming and design using Unity.

Download the app on Google Play or the App Store for more information.

New Philadelphia AR Tour

The New Philadelphia AR Tour was created by STUDIO Amakawa. The application was showcased to U.S. Senator Dick Durbin as the location became a National Historic Site. My roles included programming and design using Unity.

Download the app on Google Play or the App Store for more information.
