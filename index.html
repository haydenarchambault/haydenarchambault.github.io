About

As a Software Developer and 3D Artist at STUDIO Amakawa I create augmented reality experiences for mobile devices including Heart Mountain AR, winner of the 2022 Wyoming State Historical Society's first place award for activities. The application is based in Cody, Wyoming at the site of the Heart Mountain Relocation Center National Historic Landmark. Additionally, we created the New Philadelphia AR Tour, which was showcased to U.S. Senator Dick Durbin as the location became a National Historic Site. Furthermore, we developed 1908 Race Riot AR, a GPS based mobile application used alongside the walking tour at the Springfield 1908 Race Riot National Historic Monument located in Springfield, Illinois.

As a 3D Artist I created production ready assets at Get Dead Entertainment. As a 3D/VFX Artist and Level Designer I worked on the game Cramit's Keep, which is now available on Steam. I have also developed games for Android that are available on itch.io, and other games for desktop.

I graduated Summa Cum Laude from Fitchburg State University with a Bachelor of Science in Game Design and received the Distinguished Scholar's Award. I am an alumnus of Tabor Academy.
